OVER ( a b -- a b a )

Equivalent of 1 pick

Example:
11 12 OVER

Stack becomes:
11 12 11

-----------------------------------------------------------------------
PICK ( x0 ... xu u -- x0 ... xu x0 )

Example:
11 12 13  2 pick

Stack becomes:
11 12 13 11

See OVER

-----------------------------------------------------------------------
REPEAT immediate

See WHILE

-----------------------------------------------------------------------
WHILE immediate (BEGIN ... WHILE ... REPEAT)

Example:
: test 6 begin 1 - dup while dup . repeat drop ;
test

Output:
5 4 3 2 1
